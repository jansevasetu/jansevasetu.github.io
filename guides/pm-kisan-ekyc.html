PM Kisan eKYC Guide

Complete your PM Kisan eKYC to continue receiving ₹6000 yearly benefit under the scheme.

Why eKYC is Important?

  • Required to receive PM Kisan payments
  • Ensures identity verification
  • Prevents fraud and duplication

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Registered mobile number

Online eKYC Process

  1. Visit PM Kisan official website
  2. Click on "eKYC" option
  3. Enter Aadhaar number
  4. Enter OTP received on mobile
  5. Submit to complete eKYC
Important: If your mobile number is not linked with Aadhaar, online eKYC will not work. In that case, visit nearest CSC center for biometric verification.

Offline eKYC Process

  1. Visit nearest CSC center
  2. Provide Aadhaar details
  3. Complete biometric verification
  4. eKYC will be updated

Check eKYC Status

  1. Visit PM Kisan portal
  2. Open "Beneficiary Status"
  3. Enter Aadhaar number
  4. Check eKYC status

Common Issues

  • OTP not received → Check mobile linked with Aadhaar
  • eKYC failed → Try offline method at CSC
  • Incorrect details → Update Aadhaar information
