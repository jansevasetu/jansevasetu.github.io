PM Kisan Status Check

Check your PM Kisan payment status, installment details and eligibility.

What You Can Check

  • Payment status
  • Installment details
  • Eligibility status

Details Required

  • Aadhaar Number
  • Registered Mobile Number
  • Bank Account Number

Step-by-Step Process

  1. Visit PM Kisan official website
  2. Click on "Beneficiary Status"
  3. Enter Aadhaar / Mobile / Account Number
  4. Click on "Get Data"
  5. View your payment and status details

Common Issues

  • Payment not received → Check bank details
  • Name mismatch → Update Aadhaar details
  • Application pending → Contact local authority
