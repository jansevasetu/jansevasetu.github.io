Ayushman Bharat Card Apply Guide

Apply for Ayushman Bharat card and get free health insurance coverage up to ₹5 lakh per year.

What is Ayushman Bharat?

Ayushman Bharat is a government health insurance scheme providing free treatment up to ₹5 lakh per family per year.

Who is Eligible?

  • Low-income families
  • SECC database beneficiaries
  • Rural and urban poor households

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Ration Card
  • Mobile Number

Step-by-Step Application Process

  1. Visit nearest CSC center or hospital
  2. Provide Aadhaar and documents
  3. Verify eligibility
  4. Complete registration
  5. Get Ayushman card

Check Eligibility Online

  1. Visit Ayushman Bharat official website
  2. Enter mobile number
  3. Verify OTP
  4. Check eligibility status

Common Issues

  • Name not found → Check SECC data
  • Eligibility denied → Visit CSC center
  • Card not generated → Reapply
