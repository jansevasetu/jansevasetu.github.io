PM Kisan Apply Guide

Step-by-step process to apply for PM Kisan Samman Nidhi scheme.

Before You Apply

  • Ensure Aadhaar is linked with bank account
  • Check land records are correct
  • Mobile number should be active

Who Can Apply?

  • Small and marginal farmers
  • Must have cultivable land
  • Valid Aadhaar and bank account

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Bank Account Details
  • Land Records

Step-by-Step Application Process

  1. Visit official PM Kisan website
  2. Click on "New Farmer Registration"
  3. Enter Aadhaar number
  4. Fill personal and land details
  5. Submit application

Time Required

Application takes around 10–15 minutes online.

Success Tips

  • Use correct Aadhaar details
  • Double check bank account number
  • Keep documents ready before starting

Common Mistakes

  • Wrong Aadhaar number
  • Incorrect bank details
  • Incomplete form submission

Check Application Status

  1. Go to official website
  2. Click on "Beneficiary Status"
  3. Enter Aadhaar or mobile number
  4. Check status
