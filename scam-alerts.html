Scam Alerts & Awareness

Stay informed about common government scheme scams and learn how to protect yourself.

⚠️ Common Government Scheme Scams

Fake PM Kisan Calls

Fraudsters ask for OTP or bank details claiming to release payment.

Ayushman Card Fraud

Fake agents charge money for card creation which is free.

Loan Approval Scams

Fake Mudra loan approvals asking for processing fees.

How to Identify a Scam?

  • Government never asks for OTP or passwords
  • Official schemes are free or low-cost
  • Always verify from official websites
  • Avoid unknown calls or messages

What Should You Do?

  • Do not share personal or banking details
  • Report scam to local authorities
  • Use official government portals only
  • Stay updated through trusted platforms

Official Sources

Always verify schemes on official government portals before taking action.
