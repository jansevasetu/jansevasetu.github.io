India’s Public Scheme Knowledge Platform

JanSevaSetu is an independent public information platform that helps citizens understand government schemes, benefits, eligibility and application processes clearly and accurately.

Why JanSevaSetu Exists

Across India, many citizens are unaware of government welfare schemes or struggle to understand eligibility, documents and application processes. JanSevaSetu was created to simplify this information and make it accessible to every citizen.
