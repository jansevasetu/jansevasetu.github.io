Ayushman Bharat Yojana

Get free health insurance coverage up to ₹5 lakh per family per year under Ayushman Bharat scheme.

Overview

Ayushman Bharat (PM-JAY) is a government health insurance scheme providing free treatment up to ₹5 lakh per family annually for eligible citizens.

Key Benefits

  • ₹5 lakh health coverage per family
  • Free treatment in empanelled hospitals
  • No registration cost
  • Covers major surgeries and treatments

Who Should Apply?

  • Low-income families
  • Rural households
  • SECC database beneficiaries

Who Should NOT Apply?

  • High income individuals
  • People already covered by private insurance

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Ration Card
  • Mobile Number

How to Check Eligibility

  1. Visit official Ayushman Bharat website
  2. Enter mobile number
  3. Verify OTP
  4. Check eligibility status

Step-by-Step Application

  1. Visit nearest hospital or CSC center
  2. Provide Aadhaar details
  3. Verify eligibility
  4. Get Ayushman card

Common Mistakes

  • Not checking eligibility first
  • Incorrect Aadhaar details
  • Visiting non-approved centers

Real Example

A low-income family can receive free hospital treatment up to ₹5 lakh annually, reducing major healthcare expenses.

FAQs

  • Q: Is it free?
    Yes, completely free
  • Q: Coverage amount?
    ₹5 lakh per year
