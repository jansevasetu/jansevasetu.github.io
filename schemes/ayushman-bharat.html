Ayushman Bharat (PM-JAY)

World’s largest government-funded health insurance scheme providing free treatment up to ₹5 lakh per family.

Overview

Ayushman Bharat Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (PM-JAY) is a flagship healthcare scheme launched by the Government of India. It provides cashless and paperless treatment to eligible families across empanelled hospitals.

Key Benefits

  • Free health insurance up to ₹5 lakh per family per year
  • Cashless treatment at empanelled hospitals
  • Covers hospitalization, surgery and critical illnesses
  • Available across public and private hospitals

Eligibility

  • Families identified in SECC database
  • No restriction on family size or age
  • Economically weaker sections eligible
  • Must have valid identification details

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Ration Card
  • Mobile number
  • Family identification documents

How to Check Eligibility

  1. Visit Ayushman Bharat official website
  2. Click on "Am I Eligible"
  3. Enter mobile number and OTP
  4. Search using name or ration card

Application Process

  1. Check eligibility online
  2. Visit nearest CSC or hospital
  3. Complete verification process
  4. Get Ayushman card

Who Should Apply?

This scheme is highly beneficial for economically weaker families who cannot afford expensive medical treatments.
