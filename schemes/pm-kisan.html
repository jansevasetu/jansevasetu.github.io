PM Kisan Samman Nidhi

Get ₹6000 yearly income support directly in your bank account under PM Kisan scheme.

Overview

PM Kisan Samman Nidhi is a government scheme that provides financial assistance of ₹6000 per year to eligible farmers in three installments.

Key Benefits

  • ₹6000 per year direct transfer
  • No middlemen involved
  • Supports small and marginal farmers

Who Should Apply?

  • Small and marginal farmers
  • Farmers with cultivable land

Who Should NOT Apply?

  • Income tax payers
  • Government employees
  • Large landholders

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Bank Account
  • Land Records

Step-by-Step Application

  1. Visit official PM Kisan portal
  2. Click on New Farmer Registration
  3. Enter Aadhaar number
  4. Fill details and submit

Common Mistakes

  • Wrong bank details
  • Aadhaar not linked
  • Incorrect land records

Real Example

A small farmer with 2 acres land receives ₹2000 every 4 months directly into his bank account under this scheme.

FAQs

  • Q: How much benefit?
    ₹6000 per year
  • Q: How to check status?
    Visit PM Kisan portal
