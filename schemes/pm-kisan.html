PM Kisan Samman Nidhi

Income support scheme for farmers providing financial assistance annually.

Overview

PM Kisan Samman Nidhi is a central government scheme that provides financial support to eligible farmers. Under this scheme, farmers receive ₹6000 per year in three installments.

Key Benefits

  • ₹6000 annual financial assistance
  • Direct benefit transfer to bank account
  • Support for small and marginal farmers

Eligibility

  • Must be a farmer
  • Must have cultivable land
  • Valid Aadhaar and bank account required

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Bank Account Details
  • Land Records

Application Process

  1. Visit official PM Kisan website
  2. Register as a new farmer
  3. Fill details and upload documents
  4. Submit application
