PM Kisan Samman Nidhi

PM-Kisan is a central sector scheme launched in 2019 to provide income support to small and marginal farmers in India.

Scheme Overview

Launch Year 2019
Ministry Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare
Benefit ₹6000 per year (₹2000 in three installments)
Payment Method Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT)

Programme Impact

Total Beneficiaries Registered 11+ crore farmers
Total Installments Released 15+
Total Amount Transferred ₹3+ lakh crore
Coverage All Indian states and UTs

Beneficiary Growth

Timeline

  • 2019 — Scheme launched
  • 2020 — Nationwide expansion
  • 2021 — Aadhaar verification introduced
  • 2023 — Land record verification improvements

Eligibility

  • Small and marginal farmers
  • Must own cultivable land
  • Aadhaar linked bank account

Required Documents

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Bank Account Details
  • Land Records

Application Process

  1. Visit PM-Kisan official portal
  2. Register farmer details
  3. Verify Aadhaar and bank account
  4. Submit application

Common Issues

  • Aadhaar mismatch
  • Land record mismatch
  • Bank account linking problems

Data Sources

  • PM-Kisan Official Portal
  • Ministry of Agriculture Reports
  • Union Budget Documents
