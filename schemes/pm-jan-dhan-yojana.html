PM Jan Dhan Yojana

Open a zero balance bank account and access financial services under PM Jan Dhan scheme.

Overview

PM Jan Dhan Yojana is a national financial inclusion scheme that provides zero balance bank accounts, debit cards, insurance and direct benefit transfer facilities.

Key Benefits

  • Zero balance account
  • Free RuPay debit card
  • Accidental insurance cover
  • Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT)

Who Should Apply?

  • People without bank account
  • Low-income households
  • Rural population

Who Should NOT Apply?

  • People already having bank account

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Identity proof
  • Address proof

Step-by-Step Application

  1. Visit nearest bank branch
  2. Fill account opening form
  3. Submit documents
  4. Account will be activated

Common Mistakes

  • Incorrect Aadhaar details
  • Not linking mobile number
  • Not activating debit card

Real Example

A rural citizen can open zero balance account and receive government benefits directly in bank.

FAQs

  • Q: Is minimum balance required?
    No
  • Q: Is it free?
    Yes
