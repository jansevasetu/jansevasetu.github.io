PM Jan Dhan Yojana

Financial inclusion scheme providing zero balance bank accounts and banking access to every citizen.

Overview

Pradhan Mantri Jan Dhan Yojana (PMJDY) is a national mission to provide banking services to every household. It ensures access to savings accounts, financial services, credit, insurance and pension facilities.

Key Benefits

  • Zero balance bank account
  • Free RuPay debit card
  • Accidental insurance cover
  • Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) support

Eligibility

  • Any Indian citizen can open account
  • No minimum balance required
  • Aadhaar or valid ID needed

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Identity proof
  • Address proof

Application Process

  1. Visit nearest bank branch
  2. Fill account opening form
  3. Submit documents
  4. Account will be activated

Who Should Apply?

This scheme is essential for individuals who do not have access to banking services, especially in rural and low-income areas.
