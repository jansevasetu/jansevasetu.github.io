Pradhan Mantri Jan Dhan Yojana

PMJDY is India's national financial inclusion mission aimed at providing banking services to every household.

Scheme Overview

Launch Year 2014
Ministry Ministry of Finance
Objective Financial inclusion for all citizens
Account Type Basic savings bank account

Programme Impact

Total Accounts Opened 50+ crore
Total Deposits ₹2+ lakh crore
Rural Accounts ≈ 60%
Women Account Holders ≈ 55%

Account Growth

Key Features

  • Zero balance bank account
  • RuPay debit card
  • Accident insurance cover
  • Direct Benefit Transfer support

Eligibility

  • Any Indian citizen
  • No minimum balance required
  • Basic identity verification

Required Documents

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Voter ID / PAN / Other identity proof

Application Process

  1. Visit nearby bank branch
  2. Submit PMJDY account form
  3. Provide identity documents
  4. Account opened instantly

Common Issues

  • KYC verification delays
  • RuPay card activation issues
  • Account dormancy
