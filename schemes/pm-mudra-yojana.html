Ayushman Bharat - PMJAY

Ayushman Bharat Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (PMJAY) is India's flagship public health insurance programme providing financial protection for hospitalisation.

Scheme Overview

Launch Year 2018
Ministry Ministry of Health & Family Welfare
Coverage ₹5 lakh health cover per family per year
Target Beneficiaries Economically vulnerable families

Programme Impact

Eligible Families 10+ crore
Beneficiaries Covered 50+ crore individuals
Hospitals Empanelled 28,000+
Total Treatments 6+ crore

Healthcare Utilisation

Timeline

  • 2018 - Ayushman Bharat launched
  • 2019 - Nationwide hospital network expansion
  • 2021 - Digital health integration
  • 2023 - Ayushman Bharat Digital Mission integration

Eligibility

  • Families listed in SECC database
  • Economically vulnerable households
  • No premium required

Required Documents

  • Aadhaar card
  • Family identification proof
  • Ayushman card

Application Process

  1. Check eligibility on PMJAY portal
  2. Visit empanelled hospital or CSC centre
  3. Generate Ayushman card
  4. Receive treatment cashlessly

Common Issues

  • Name mismatch in database
  • Hospital empanelment limitations
  • Card generation delays
