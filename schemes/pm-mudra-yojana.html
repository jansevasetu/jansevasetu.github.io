PM Mudra Yojana

Get business loans up to ₹10 lakh without collateral under PM Mudra scheme.

Overview

PM Mudra Yojana (PMMY) provides financial support to small businesses, startups and self-employed individuals through banks and NBFCs.

Loan Categories

  • Shishu: Up to ₹50,000
  • Kishore: ₹50,000 – ₹5 lakh
  • Tarun: ₹5 lakh – ₹10 lakh

Key Benefits

  • No collateral required
  • Easy loan approval
  • Supports startups and small businesses
  • Flexible repayment options

Who Should Apply?

  • Small business owners
  • Self-employed individuals
  • Street vendors
  • Startups

Who Should NOT Apply?

  • Large corporations
  • Individuals without business plans

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Business details
  • Bank account details

Step-by-Step Application

  1. Visit bank or apply online
  2. Select loan category
  3. Submit documents
  4. Loan approval and disbursement

Common Mistakes

  • No proper business plan
  • Incorrect documents
  • Choosing wrong loan category

Real Example

A small shop owner can get ₹50,000–₹5 lakh loan to expand business under Mudra scheme.

FAQs

  • Q: Is collateral required?
    No
  • Q: Max loan amount?
    ₹10 lakh
