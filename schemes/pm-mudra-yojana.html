PM Mudra Yojana

Loan scheme to support small businesses, startups and self-employed individuals.

Overview

Pradhan Mantri Mudra Yojana (PMMY) provides loans to micro and small businesses through banks and financial institutions. It helps individuals start or expand their business activities.

Loan Categories

  • Shishu: Loans up to ₹50,000
  • Kishore: ₹50,000 to ₹5 lakh
  • Tarun: ₹5 lakh to ₹10 lakh

Key Benefits

  • No collateral required for loans
  • Supports small businesses and startups
  • Flexible repayment options
  • Available through banks, NBFCs and MFIs

Eligibility

  • Indian citizen
  • Small business owner or entrepreneur
  • Non-corporate and non-farm sector
  • Valid business plan required

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Business details
  • Bank account details
  • Address proof

Application Process

  1. Visit bank or apply online
  2. Select loan category
  3. Submit application and documents
  4. Loan approval and disbursement

Who Should Apply?

This scheme is ideal for small business owners, shopkeepers, startups and self-employed individuals who need financial support.
