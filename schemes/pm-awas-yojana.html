PM Awas Yojana

Get government support to build or buy your own house under PMAY scheme.

Overview

Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY) is a housing scheme that helps eligible citizens build or buy affordable homes with government subsidy.

Key Benefits

  • Interest subsidy on home loan
  • Affordable housing support
  • Support for urban and rural areas

Who Should Apply?

  • Low-income families
  • First-time home buyers
  • People without permanent houses

Who Should NOT Apply?

  • People already owning a pucca house
  • High income individuals

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Income certificate
  • Bank details
  • Property details

Step-by-Step Application

  1. Visit PMAY official website
  2. Select Urban or Rural scheme
  3. Fill application form
  4. Submit documents

Common Mistakes

  • Incorrect income details
  • Property ownership confusion
  • Incomplete documents

Real Example

A low-income family can get subsidy on home loan and reduce overall cost of owning a house.

FAQs

  • Q: Who gets benefit?
    Low income families
  • Q: What is subsidy?
    Interest reduction on loan
