Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY)

Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana is a flagship housing scheme launched by the Government of India to provide affordable housing to economically weaker sections of society.

Launch Year

2015

Implementing Ministry

Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs

Objective

The objective of PMAY is to provide affordable housing and ensure “Housing for All”.

Benefits

  • Interest subsidy on home loans
  • Financial assistance for house construction
  • Support for economically weaker sections

Eligibility

  • Economically Weaker Section (EWS)
  • Low Income Group (LIG)
  • Applicant should not own a permanent house

Required Documents

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Income certificate
  • Bank account details
  • Address proof

Application Process

Applicants can apply through the official PMAY website or authorized housing authorities.

Official Website

https://pmaymis.gov.in

Helpline

PMAY Helpline: 1800-11-6446

FAQ

Q: Who can apply for PM Awas Yojana?

Eligible families belonging to economically weaker sections or low income groups who do not own a permanent house.
