PM Awas Yojana (PMAY)

Affordable housing scheme to provide pucca houses with basic facilities to eligible citizens.

Overview

Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY) is a flagship housing scheme of the Government of India aimed at providing affordable housing to all eligible families. It focuses on economically weaker sections (EWS), low-income groups (LIG) and middle-income groups (MIG).

Key Benefits

  • Subsidy on home loan interest (CLSS)
  • Financial assistance for house construction
  • Focus on affordable housing for all
  • Priority to women ownership in many cases

Eligibility

  • Applicant should not own a pucca house
  • Belongs to EWS, LIG or MIG category
  • Valid Aadhaar and income proof required
  • Family must not have availed housing scheme earlier

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Income Certificate
  • Bank Account Details
  • Property documents

Application Process

  1. Visit official PMAY website
  2. Select beneficiary category
  3. Fill application form
  4. Upload documents
  5. Submit and track status

Who Should Apply?

This scheme is most useful for low-income families, urban poor and individuals who do not own a permanent house.
