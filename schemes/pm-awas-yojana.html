Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana

PMAY aims to provide affordable housing for economically weaker sections and low income families in India.

Scheme Overview

Launch Year 2015
Ministry Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs
Objective Housing for All
Type Housing Subsidy Programme

Programme Impact

Houses Sanctioned 3+ crore
Houses Completed 2+ crore
Total Investment ₹10+ lakh crore
Coverage Urban & Rural India

Housing Progress

Timeline

  • 2015 — PMAY launched
  • 2016 — Credit linked subsidy introduced
  • 2018 — Urban housing expansion
  • 2022 — Housing for all mission target

Eligibility

  • Economically weaker section
  • Low income families
  • No permanent house ownership

Required Documents

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Income Certificate
  • Address Proof

Application Process

  1. Visit PMAY portal
  2. Submit housing application
  3. Verify income & identity
  4. Receive subsidy approval

Common Issues

  • Income verification delay
  • Loan approval issues
  • Document mismatch
