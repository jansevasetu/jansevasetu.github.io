About JanSevaSetu

Bridging the gap between citizens and government welfare schemes across India.

What is JanSevaSetu?

JanSevaSetu is a public welfare intelligence platform designed to help Indian citizens easily discover, understand and access government schemes.

Instead of navigating multiple government portals, users can explore schemes, check eligibility and find relevant opportunities in one place.

Why JanSevaSetu Exists

Millions of citizens miss out on government benefits simply because they are unaware or confused about eligibility and application processes.

JanSevaSetu was created to simplify this complexity and make welfare information accessible, clear and actionable.

What Makes It Different

  • Structured and simplified scheme information
  • Smart discovery tools (Scheme Finder)
  • Step-by-step citizen guides
  • Scam awareness and public protection
  • Category-based navigation

Our Mission

To ensure that every citizen of India can access the benefits they are entitled to without confusion or barriers.

Our Vision

To become India’s most trusted platform for public welfare intelligence and citizen empowerment.

Who Can Use This Platform?

  • Farmers
  • Students
  • Women
  • Job seekers
  • Entrepreneurs
  • General citizens
